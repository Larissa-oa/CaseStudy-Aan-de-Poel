Aan de Poel

Discover the culinary maestry of Restaurant Aan de Poel, where Chef Stefan van Sprang creates unforgettable dining experiences and unique flavors.
Located by the beautiful Poel in Amstelveen, this restaurant has been a favorite destination for food lovers since 2007. Their dedication to excellence was recognized in 2013 when they received two Michelin stars, a proud achievement they maintain to this day.
Chef van Sprang's passion for fresh, seasonal ingredients shines through in every dish and the menu celebrates the best of local flavors, crafted with care and creativity. Experience the taste of Aan de Poel at home, or visit their elegant dining room for a special evening. Stay connected with Restaurant Aan de Poel by subscribing to their newsletter. You'll be the first to know about their latest creations, special events, and new featured products.
